Who are we?

Anara is a platforn for a daily meal kit in an effort to prevent Non-communicable Diseases in Indonesia. We have a vision and mission that are our foundation in making Anara, namely:

* Making Anara a platform that makes cooking easier for Indonesias
* Improe the quality of lifeof the community, especially those with non-communicable diseases
* Improve the welfare of local farmers

We aim to contribute to educating the public and spreading the positive impact of cooking and consuming healthy food so that it can prevent and reduce the number of deaths caused by various non-communicable diaseases in Indonesia.
